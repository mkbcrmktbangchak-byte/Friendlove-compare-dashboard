Key Insights — Week-over-Week
+16%
Strong Volume Growth — All Metrics Up
Redemptions +16.1%, Unique Customers +14.9%, Net Sales +15.3%. The campaign is building momentum week-over-week. No sign of fatigue yet.
D1
Week 2 Peaked Earlier on Day 1
Day 1 of Wk2 (2,090) was 51% higher than Day 1 of Wk1 (1,384). Growing customer awareness means more people are redeeming immediately on Day 1 rather than waiting.
8AM
Peak Hour Shifted Earlier: 09:00 → 08:00
Wk2 peak was 08:00 (1,291) vs Wk1 09:00 (848). Volume also 52% higher at peak. Morning rush is intensifying — ensure staff and stock readiness by 07:30.
+68
67 More Shops Participating in Week 2
828 shops active vs 761 in Wk1. Participation rate improved from 65.0% to 70.8% (+5.8pp). Momentum is building but still 342 shops (29.2%) remain inactive vs 80% target.
฿80
Avg. Ticket Stable at ~฿80
฿79.9 in Wk2 vs ฿80.4 in Wk1 — essentially flat (−0.6%). Sales growth is purely volume-driven. Customers are not spending more per visit — promotion is attracting additional visits, not upsell.
49%
Discount Rate Unchanged at ~49.8%
Despite volume growth, discount rate holds at 49.8% (vs 49.7% in Wk1). Total discount cost grew proportionally +16.1%. Cap mechanism needs review before campaign extends further.
COCO
COCO Redemptions Slightly Declined
COCO dropped from 1,115 to 1,056 uses (−5.3%) while overall volume grew +16.1%. COCO's share fell from 24.5% to 20.0%. Targeted support for COCO stores recommended.
CTR
Click→Redeem +6.9pp — Stronger Buyer Intent
Week 2 Click→Redeem jumped from 27.6% to 34.5% despite CTR falling (6.2%→5.6%). Fewer but more intent-driven clicks. Revenue per Click rose +24% (฿22.2→฿27.5). Consider optimising message to attract more high-intent clickers.
FZ
FZ Redemptions Up +21.2% WoW
FZ stores grew from 3,111 to 3,772 redemptions (+661, +21.2%) — strongest growth across all formats. FZ share increased from 68.3% to 71.4%, reinforcing its dominant role in campaign performance.
