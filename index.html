Key Insights & Recommendations
📈
Wk2 Outperformed Wk1 by +16.1%
Week 2 delivered 5,284 redemptions vs 4,553 in Week 1 — a +16.1% increase. Net Sales grew from ฿365,864 to ฿422,008 ex.vat, demonstrating campaign learning effect.
🏆
Peak Day: 22 Jun — 2,090 Redemptions
Campaign's single best day was Wk2 Day 1 (22 Jun) at 2,090 uses. The first-day urgency pattern was consistent in both weeks — Day 1 and Day 2 drive the majority of volume.
☕
Coffee Dominates at 58% of Sales
Americano Iced accounts for 4,700 redemptions (47.8% of total). Tea (24.9%) and Cocoa (11.6%) are stable but underrepresented — targeted push could shift mix.
🕘
Peak Hour: 08:00 (1,944 Redemptions)
Morning rush 07:00–10:00 accounts for 4,948 redemptions (50.3% of total). Staff readiness in this window is critical. Late-day volume drops sharply after 13:00.
🏪
255 Shops Still Had Zero Redemptions
915 of 1,170 selected shops participated (78.2%). Remaining 255 shops had no redemptions — investigate location coverage, customer profile fit, and staff training gaps.
⚠️
Discount Rate ~49.7% — Needs Review
Average discount is 38.0 THB (ex.vat) per redemption — significantly above face value. Discount stacking logic should be reviewed and capping enforced before future campaigns.
👥
22.7% of Customers Returned for Week 2
1,710 of 7,547 unique members redeemed in both Wk1 and Wk2 — a strong cross-week retention signal. Within each individual week, multi-use rate was 5.4% (Wk1) and 5.9% (Wk2), consistent across the campaign.
🎯
Click → Redeem 30.9% — Campaign Best
Combined rate improved to 30.9% vs 27.6% in Wk1. Offer quality is strong and UX is effective. Primary opportunity: raise CTR from 5.9% → 10% to unlock ~+22,000 more redemptions at zero extra send cost.
